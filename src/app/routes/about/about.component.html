WHY VietNam

New destination for software outsourcing

  • Vietnam is an Asia Tier 1 emerging-market location and top 5 location for outsourcing (Gartner’s Leading Global Locations for Offshore Services, 2016)
  • Ho Chi Minh City in Top Outsourcing Cities in 8 consecutive years (Tholons, 2009-2016)
  • Vietnam is #3 in global offshoring hotspot and #1 in competitive cost (Tower Watson, Services Offshoring Ranking 2013)
  • Vietnam is ranked 1 in pioneering location and cost environment (Cushman & Wakefield business process outsourcing and shaped service location index, 2016-2017)
  • Vietnam is #6 in 2017 Global Services Location Index (A.T. Kerney, 2017)
  • Vietnam is an offshoring destination most preferable by Japanese companies (Resorz Co., Ltd, 2016)

Large talent pool for software industry

  • Vietnam is in top 10 countries with most engineering graduates – research of World Economic Forum in 2015 on 128 countries (Forbes)
  • Supply will continue to exceed demand for many years
  • Nearly 130,000 Vietnamese students are studying abroad: 6th in USA, 5rd in Australia, 2nd in Japan (Australian Embassy Vietnam, University World News, Japan Student Services Organization（JASSO), 2017)

Vietnam IT Outsourcing Conference 2017 (VNITO 2017 )

The VNITO Conference 2017 will be held in Ho Chi Minh City, the Vietnam’s largest city, from 19th to 20th of October at GEM Center. The two-day event aims to attract more than 300 multi-national and high-tech companies and IT universities. The goal of VNITO Conference 2017 is to link Vietnamese software outsourcing companies and potential overseas technology companies, and promote Vietnam as an attractive technology outsourcing destination.

Joining the conference, participants will have a chance to know about:

  • Vietnam IT industry information and data, business and investment environment in Vietnam
  • Human resources, education & training, labor market, infrastructure, etc.
  • Experiences and case studies in working with Vietnamese companies
  • Business matching and visiting top Vietnam ITO and BPO companies
  • Opportunities to meet, exchange and find the potential business partners

Any company looking for IT services and talents in VN can find great information from 2 days event with many activities:

  • Conference with latest data and insights
  • 4 panel discussions with many useful topics
  • Business matching with top IT VN companies
  • Technology and software park tour
  • Gala dinner
  • City tour

All above activities are free of charge for overseas participants.

For more event details, please access: http://vnito2017.vnito.org/
